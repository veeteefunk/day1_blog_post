Day # 1: Homepage

Hi everyone, my name is Victor Marin Viloria and I have decided to dedicate my entire summer to coding. I needed to refresh some web programming fundamental concepts, and this project also gives me the opportunity to design exercises for everyone who is beginning his/her coding journey.


I am going to make a website each day along with providing a mini-lesson on the concepts I have had to study and implement. The posts on this blog will contain these lessons with a couple of exercises for you to do and practice.


I am a true believer in the power of consistency, so I really hope this project helps me get back in programming shape along with your help. Your feedback is important, so if you ever think that I could have done something better let me know!


This project starts today and I hope you will join on this adventure. I am sure this will help me become a better developer, writer and teacher. I am sure this will be hard to pull off, I know I'll get frustrated, but in the end my vision remains fixed on my one and only goal: to become a better person.
